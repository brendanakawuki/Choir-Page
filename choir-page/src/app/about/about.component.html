What we Aim at

  • Celebrating God’s presence in highest praise and dynamic worship.

  • Proclaiming God’s Word, demonstrating God’s power, and empowering God’s people.

  • Building confident, committed and effective leaders.

  • Valuing and empowering the men and women in our Church.

  • Mobilising our Church for ministry and mission.

  • Being committed to provide educational, social and spiritual development for our people, including mentoring opportunities and disability awareness.

  • Developing our Church building to provide a variety of safe and reliable services, pertinent to our corporate vision.

  • To help train members and create employment for them in the cultural industry, tour managers and event producers.

  • To tour the choir nationally and internationally.
